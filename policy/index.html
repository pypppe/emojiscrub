Privacy Policy

How we use your data.

We do not collect your data, cookies, etc..., we don't know what you do on the site, what words you guess, or anything. Your data is private and isn't used to improve performance, improve easier or harder words based on guesses etc...

Cache

When you press Skip on the load menu, create an account, or even change settings, that is saved via Localstorage to remember, if you wish to clear all cache please click here.

Ads & Promotions

There are no ads or promotions in Emojiscrub, we believe ADS & promotions ruin the fun/experience for anyone. We don't make any money from this site, and you shouldn't be forced to watch ADS for a game where you guess words, this game is created for anyone to enjoy.

FAQ

How do I clear specific cache?

You sort of can, if you wish to log out you have to press Log Out on the account page, you cannot remove the cache for loading screen unless you clear all cache, and you can reset your Settings by pressing Reset Settings.

Is this game open-source?

Yes, this game is fully open-source and the Electron version provides the code with no modifications such as obsfucation, to view the GitHub page, click here.
