Privacy Policy

How we use your data

Your privacy is taken seriously. Emojiscrub does not collect, track, store, or analyze any personal information. We do not monitor your gameplay, record guessed words, or observe how you interact with the site. Your activity remains entirely private and is never used for analytics, performance optimization, or difficulty adjustments.

Cache

Certain preferences—such as skipping the loading screen, account actions, or customized settings—are saved locally on your device using Local Storage. This allows Emojiscrub to remember your choices for a smoother experience. If you wish to remove all locally stored data, you may do so at any time by clicking here.

Ads & Promotions

Emojiscrub is proudly free of advertisements and promotional content. We believe ads interrupt immersion and diminish the enjoyment of a game designed to be simple and fun. The project generates no revenue, and players are never required to watch ads to play. However, advertisements present in the mobile version are outside of our control and cannot currently be removed.

FAQ

How do I clear specific cache?

Some data can be cleared individually. Logging out from the Account page will remove account-related information, and settings can be restored using the Reset Settings option. The loading screen preference can only be removed by clearing all cache.

Is this game open-source?

Yes. Emojiscrub is fully open-source. The Electron version includes the complete source code with no obfuscation. You can view the repository on GitHub by clicking here.
